About me

I am Nishan Bastola, a Bachelor's student in Electronics and Communication Engineering with a strong passion for data science. My goal is to bridge the gap between engineering and data science, leveraging data-driven insights to create innovative solutions. Hobbies and Interest

As an Electronics and Communication Engineering student, I have a strong interest in exploring real-life projects that integrate technology and data science. I enjoy working on IoT (Internet of Things) devices, utilizing sensors and microcontrollers to collect and analyze data for meaningful insights. I actively participate in Kaggle competitions, where I apply my data science skills to solve real-world problems through data analysis and machine learning. This platform allows me to collaborate with a global community of data enthusiasts and continually enhance my skills through practical challenges. I’m particularly fascinated by how data can enhance everyday applications, such as smart home systems and wearable technology. Through personal projects, I focus on data visualization and predictive modeling, seeking to push the boundaries of how electronics and data science can work together to create impactful solutions.
